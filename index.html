Post a Gig

Students can submit their service using a parent-approved form. All submissions are reviewed before being posted.

  • First Name
  • Service Category

Listings will not be posted without parent or guardian approval.

Safety Rules

  • Parent or guardian permission is required
  • No sharing of home addresses or personal details
  • Adult awareness is required for in-person meetings
  • Payments are handled off-site with parents involved
  • Unsafe listings will be removed
